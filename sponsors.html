OUR SPONSORS

Listed below are the brands that continuously support the Utah Climbing Team. Their contributions have created many opportunities for our team, and for that, we are very thankful. Brands interested in supporting our team are welcome to email us utahclimbingteam@gmail.com.

PUSHER LLC

Pusher is a Salt Lake City–based company that has been pioneering the climbing industry since the early ’90s. They are most widely known for their production of climbing holds, but more recently, they have expanded into designing unique t-shirts and prints that embody the old-school spirit of climbing in the U.S. They have been very generous in supporting our team by allowing us to hold practices at their HQ and by helping print our team jerseys and merch. Check out their website to learn more! https://pusher.world/
