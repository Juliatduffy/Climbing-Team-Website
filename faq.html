FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • WHO CAN JOIN?

      All University of Utah students, male or female, undergraduate or graduate, are qualified for membership.

  • HOW MUCH DOES IT COST?

      $50 in team dues for rec team, $200 in team dues for comp team.

  • WHEN AND WHERE ARE TEAM PRACTICES?

      Our team holds practice once a week at the Bouldering Project Salt Lake City. Practice days for this school year have yet to be determined.

  • ARE THERE TRYOUTS?

      No! You do not have to try out to join either of our teams. We do require that all members pay the fees outlined above and attend certain events throughout the school year. Mandatory events are outlined on the events page.

  • WHAT ARE THE OUTDOOR TRIPS LIKE?

      Information about outdoor trips can be found on this google doc. Also, check out this video from our fall break trip by Aidan Cooley: "Red Rock Rumble With the Utah School of Climb"

  • WHAT BENEFITS COME WITH REC TEAM MEMBERSHIP?

      Recreational team members will have access to gym membership discounts, gear discounts, weekly team practices, team outdoor trips, and special events throughout the school.

  • WHAT BENEFITS COME WITH COMP TEAM MEMBERSHIP?

      Competition team members receive all of the same benefits that recreational team members receive. In addition to these benefits, competition team members will receive jerseys and gear at no additional cost. Additionally, comp team will have access to several competition specific training events throughout the semester. Lastly, competition members will be provided housing at the national championships and can apply for competition funding.

  • HOW CAN I SUPPORT THE TEAM?

      You can donate here and follow us on our socials at the bottom of this page!
