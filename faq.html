FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • Who Can Join? - All University of Utah students, undergraduate or graduate, are qualified for membership.

  • What If I Have Never Climbed Before? - If you have never tried climbing before but think you might enjoy it, our Recreational Team is a great place to start. At our weekly team practices, new climbers can get together in a safe environment to learn the fundamentals of climbing. We have two experienced coaches who will be at practice to help with this. Additionally, there are plenty of great climbers on our team who would be sure to give you some pointers if you asked.

  • How Much Does It Cost? - $50 in team dues for rec team, $150 in team dues for comp team.

  • Are There Tryouts? - No! You do not have to try out to join either of our teams. We do require that all members pay the fees outlined above.

  • When and Where are Team Practices? - Our team holds practice once a week at the Bouldering Project Salt Lake City. Practice days for this school year have yet to be determined.

  • What are the Outdoor Trips Like? - Information about outdoor trips can be found on this google doc. Also, check out this video from our last fall break trip by Aidan Cooley: "Red Rock Rumble"

  • Why Join Rec Team? - Recreational team members will have access to gym membership discounts, gear discounts, weekly team practices, team outdoor trips, and special events throughout the school year. Also, joining rec team is a great way to meet climbers of your ability level and improve your climbing skills.

  • Why Join Comp Team? - Comp team members receive all of the benefits that rec team members receive. In addition to these benefits, comp team members will be provided with jerseys and gear (e.g. harnesses and chalk bags) for competitions. There will be team housing for comp team members at the national championships and comp team members will be eligible to apply for competition funding.
