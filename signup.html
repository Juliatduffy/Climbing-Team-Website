HOW TO JOIN (REC TEAM)

  • 1. Venmo $50 to @Campbell_Sarinopoulos (If you don't have Venmo shoot us an email at utahclimbingteam@gmail.com and we can figure out another way for you to pay).
  • 2. Fill out the rec team sign up google form.
  • 3. Follow us on Instagram! We will email a Discord invite link to members who fill out the above form and pay team dues.

HOW TO JOIN (COMP TEAM)

  • 1. Venmo $150 to @Campbell_Sarinopoulos (If you don't have Venmo shoot us an email at utahclimbingteam@gmail.com and we can figure out another way for you to pay).
  • 2. Fill out the comp team sign up google form.
  • 3. Follow us on Instagram! We will email a Discord invite link to members who fill out the above form and pay team dues.
  • 4. See the remaining steps outlined in the Competition Team info sheet.
  • Note: If you are contemplating joining the comp team, you are allowed to join the rec team first and decide later. That said, we do recommend signing up sooner rather than later so we can get a better headcount of who is interested.
