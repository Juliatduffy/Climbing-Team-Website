  1. COLLEGIATE NATIONALS QUALIFICATION EVENT (CNQE)

Qualification for the CNQE is achieved by competing in a QE or Recognized event.

  • All QE participants will be qualified in both boulder and lead disciplines at a CNQE.
  • Proof of Enrollment is required at registration.
  • Must have a Collegiate Membership
  • May compete at a CNQE anywhere in the country and are limited to competing at one single CNQE
  • A single registration will register you for both boulder and lead (Adv)/Top rope (Int), though you are not required to compete in both disciplines.
  • At registration, competitors must choose Advanced or Intermediate category. Non-Binary is not an option this year but we are looking to add it in subsequent years.

  1. TEAM ROSTER

Collegiate Team Rosters open October 2024.  This will be used for Team Championship awards at National Championship.

According to Rule 3.11.4 b), at a Collegiate Championship competition, the team’s three (3) highest ranked eligible team member competitors in the Discipline in each Category shall be awarded team ranking points. For this purpose, there are four (4) eligible categories which shall be awarded team ranking points including Men’s and Women's Advanced and Men’s and Women’s Intermediate.

To create a Team Roster, refer to the Guide to Collegiate Team Roster Setup.

  1. TEAM PAYMENT

As of October 18, 2023, you will be able to pay for team discount codes in your USA Climbing Account. Please allocate an appropriate amount of time to complete this process.

Steps

  1. Log into your USA Climbing Member Portal.
  2. Use the Profile dropdown and choose Discount Codes
  3. Click on Add Discount Codes button
  4. Choose the discount code increment ($5 to $100)
  5. In Discount Code Details, choose the quantity needed (1 to 10)
  6. Repeat Steps 4 to 5 as needed
  7. Checkout and pay

Once you’ve received the confirmation email, forward it madeleine@usaclimbing.org with the following information so we can generate your discount codes:

  • Collegiate Division
  • School Name
  • Membership Quantity
  • Qualification Event Name and Quantity

Team Distributes Codes to Members

The school will distribute the codes generated by USA Climbing to its members. For members to receive the discount, codes must be applied during the member’s checkout.

Schools are also able to create a USA Climbing Account to pay for team discount codes.
