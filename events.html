SPRING SIGN-UPS OPEN

Spring sign-ups open on January 6th. If you paid dues and signed up for team in the fall, you do not need to register again for the spring. See the sign-up page for more details.

Weekly Rec Team Practice

● Wednesdays from 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM
● 1/8/25 - 4/30/25
● Momentum Millcreek

Rec team practice is a place for all of us to climb and train together and have a good time. The workouts are optional and scalable. Comp team members are encouraged to attend. Those who don't have a Momentum membership can sign up for a free practice day pass here. This form opens at 9:00 AM on the Thursday before each practice.

Weekly Comp Team Practice

This Spring, comp team practice will be held on Thursdays and alternate between Momentum Millcreek from 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM and the Bouldering Project Salt Lake from 6:00 - 8:00 PM.

Competition team practices will be facilitated by Gavin Albright, Sophia Hoermann and Soren Callo. These practices will be smaller and more focused than the rec team practices.

Monthly Speed Clinics With Albert Ok

● Speed Clinic Dates: January 15th, February 12th, March 19th*, April 23rd*
● Open Speed Session Dates: January 22nd, February 19th, March 26th, April 13th*

Olympic-level coach Albert Ok is leading series of free monthly speed climbing clinics for our team this spring. Each clinic will be followed by an open speed wall session the following week so that athletes can practice what they learned in the clinic. Some dates are subject to change (*). These clinics will be held on the Momentum Millcreek speed wall from 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM.

Monthly Training Center Sessions

● Tentative Dates: January 27th, February 20th, March 22nd, April 2nd
● 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM
● USA Climbing Training Center

This spring, we are planning to host a monthly Comp Team Training Center Session. These sessions will be less structured than our training camps but will still be a great opportunity to receive coaching on competition style boulders. Access to the TC costs $20/person per session.

In addition to these monthly TC days, we are doing a two-day Comp Team Training Camp in April. More details about these training opportunities will be released soon!

Front Membership Registration Windows

Our membership deals with the Front climbing club are only available during the following registration windows: January 13th - 20th, March 17th - 23rd, August 25th - September 1st, and October 13th - 20th. To set up your membership at the Front, you will first need to register online at this website: https://thefrontclimbingclub.com/prodeal/

Under "SLC" and the "Corporate" option, select "New Member" and then fill in your information. Then, next time you go to the gym, you can ask the front desk staff to finish setting up your membership. For more details on membership pricing (for the Front, Momentum, and the Bouldering Project), see the pricing breakdown on the rec team page.
