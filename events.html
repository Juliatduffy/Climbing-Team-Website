Spring Break Trip

● March 8th - 16th
● Red Rocks, Nevada

For Spring break this year, we will be going to Red Rocks, Nevada. There will be two airbnbs, each will cost $5 / night for team members and $10/ night for non-members. To stay at one of these airbnbs, please fill out the sign up form in our team discord, on the #outdoor-trips channel. If you aren't on team but would like to stay with us, just shoot us an email. Red rocks is a great climbing area for all abilities and all disciplines (trad, sport, bouldering). We hope to see you there :) As always, specifics about the trip can be found on Discord.

Weekly Comp Team Practice

This Spring, comp team practice will be held on Thursdays and alternate between Momentum Millcreek from 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM and the Bouldering Project Salt Lake from 6:00 - 8:00 PM.

Competition team practices will be facilitated by Gavin Albright, Sophia Hoermann and Soren Callo. These practices will be smaller and more focused than the rec team practices.
