DONATE

Donations are greatly appreciated and directly support our team. We are a self-supported organization, and each contribution, no matter the size, makes a huge impact. Donations will be used to:

  • Fund Competitions: To subsidize travel, lodging, and registration fees for our athletes.
  • Organize Events: To keep our events (e.g. outdoor trips and training camps) free for our members.
  • Increase Access: To waive membership fees and provide equipment to students who need it.
