Welcome to the Climbing Team at the University of Utah! We are a student-run organization with a goal to connect, challenge, and inspire students through climbing. We have both a recreational team and a competitive team. In 2022, 2023 and 2024, our competitive team took first place in the USA Climbing Collegiate National Championships.
